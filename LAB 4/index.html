In crafting the CSS for this webpage, several deliberate choices were made to enhance readability, maintain consistency, and provide a visually appealing presentation. Let's delve into the reasoning behind these stylistic decisions:

  3. Color Choices: Color plays a crucial role in conveying information and establishing visual appeal. For the main headings (H1), a vibrant blue (#007bff) was chosen to command attention and create a focal point. Subheadings (H2 and H3) are styled in a deep red (#dc3545), offering a subtle contrast against the background while maintaining readability. The body text is set to a standard dark gray (#333), ensuring optimal contrast against the white background for easy reading.

  4. Margin and Padding Choices: Consistent margin and padding settings were applied throughout the document to maintain visual balance and improve readability. The entire document is enclosed within an article element, which has a max-width of 800px and balanced padding (20px), ensuring that content remains centered and easily readable on various screen sizes. Margin-bottom and padding-bottom were added to headings and paragraphs to create appropriate spacing between elements, preventing visual clutter and enhancing readability.

These stylistic choices were made with the aim of creating a visually appealing, easy-to-read, and professional-looking resume.
